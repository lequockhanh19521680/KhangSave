OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR > INSPECTION

Xem Phần Chuẩn Bị Kích chuột vào đây



1. INSPECT OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY LH



  1. Disconnect the mirror connector.

  1. Apply battery voltage and check the operation of the mirror.

    OK:
    Measurement Condition
    		Specified Condition
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 5 (MV)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 3 (M+)
    		Turns upward (A)
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 3 (M+)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 5 (MV)
    		Turns downward (B)
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 1 (MH)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 3 (M+)
    		Turns left (C)
    Battery positive (+) → Terminal 3 (M+)
    Battery negative (-) → Terminal 1 (MH)
    		Turns right (D)

    If the result is not as specified, replace the mirror assembly.
