System Outline

When the ignition SW is turned to ON position, the current from the MET fuse flows to TERMINAL 21 of the combination meter.

Seat Belt Warning System

When the ignition SW turned on, a signal is input to the combination meter. To determine whether the driver has fastened the seat belt, a signal is input from the front seat inner belt (Driver’s side) to TERMINAL 11 of the combination meter. When the seat belt is not fastened, the driver’s seat belt warning light in the combination meter blinks.
