1. Zoom In and Zoom Out

Buttons
  • [Zoom-In]: Zoom in a diagram.
  • [Zoom-Out]: Zoom out a diagram.
Operations Using Keyboard and Mouse

    Place the mouse cursor somewhere in the window screen and perform either of the following operations:

  • [Zoom-In]: Hold down the [Ctrl] key and click the left mouse button.
  • [Zoom-Out]: Hold down the [Ctrl] + [Shift] keys and click the left mouse button

2. Keyword Search --- Quick Result with a Connector Name

2.1 Select [Chart/List] - [Connector List] from the Main Menu.
2.2 Search with a Keyword.
Enter the keyword to the text box [A], and click the search button [B].
2.3 Select the Connector Name.
Select the connector name in [D].
2.4 Move to the System Circuit.
Click the link [F]"Engine Control"
2.5 The Related System Circuit will be Present, the Corresponding Parts will be Highlighted.

3. Find "Unknown-Location Item" in the Routing Diagram Quickly

3.1 Click one of the parts in the wiring diagram.
3.2 The corresponded connector will be displayed in the lower left of the screen. Click the link shown below the connector.
3.3 You Can Obtain the Corresponded Routing Diagram and Get the Exact Location of It".
